Fully Deployed Live S&P 500 Pipeline

I've succesfully implemented an pipeline that collects data on Market Symbol, Time of Trades, Trade Volume, Current Price, Day Change, and Trade Conditions for every company in the S&P 500, approximately every 15 minutes. This data is moved from https://finnhub.io websocket into a postgreSQL database using a simple Airflow DAG. For now, I'm working on building a live, deployed web dashboard which allows users to explore the effect of volume of trade price. I hope to add some kind of Trump data to allow exploring the effect of Trump on the S&P 500 as a whole and perhaps individual stocks. So far I have collected +4 million rows of data. I will be trying to integrate PySpark throughout to speed up the pipeline, and certainly in the visualizing and modeling phase. I would also like to have the pipeline automatically connected to a ML/DL platform like Sagemaker and a BI platform like Tableau, as I think a good pipline should have multiple tranformation paths for different endpoints.
